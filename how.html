How it works

How FateDate works.

FateDate is a structured dating system designed to move people from discovery → connection → real-world meeting → outcome, with clarity, safety and intention.

It is not based on endless messaging or passive swiping. Instead, it offers multiple guided ways to connect, followed by structured real-world progression.

Trust, identity & privacy

Identity verification.

ID verification

Every member is ID-verified before they can interact with others.

To strengthen authenticity, users may also upload a recent timestamped photo to confirm they are a real, current identity.

A trust level system provides ongoing transparency based on consistency and behaviour across the platform.

Private profiles

Users begin with private profiles shown to non-connections. This includes:

  • Limited profile visibility to non-connected users
  • Ability to keep real name private until connection
  • Controlled profile discovery (photos, updates, interests)

Once two users connect

  • Full profiles unlock
  • Real names become visible
  • Deeper interaction becomes available

Users can also like photos, engage with profile updates, and interact with shared content before meeting.

How people connect

Multiple ways to connect, not standard matching.

FateDate is built around multiple ways to connect, not standard matching. Users can discover and engage through a growing library of interaction styles, including:

Connection type 01

FateThread

A progressive reveal connection experience.
  • Users learn about each other gradually
  • Information is revealed step-by-step
  • A visual identity "builds" over time
  • The final stage reveals the profile photo

This is designed to build curiosity, depth and emotional understanding before appearance-led judgement.

Connection type 02

FatePull

A preference-driven discovery system.
  • Users "pull" traits, prompts, interests or signals they like
  • These pulls connect them with relevant users
  • Connection is based on interest attraction rather than passive swiping

Users can pull anything they find interesting about a profile or prompt.

Connection type 03

Out & About Mode

A real-world discovery layer for missed moments.
  • See people you may have crossed paths with
  • Discover encounters that could have gone somewhere
  • Turn real-world proximity into meaningful connection

Designed for the moments that felt like fate.

Connection type 04

HiddenFate

A time-pressured blind date experience. Flow
  • Two users are connected by FateDate for a private chat
  • They have 48 hours to plan a date using DateDiary
  • If no date is planned within 48 hours → the connection expires
  • If a date is planned → it becomes a real-world booking
Purpose
  • Removes endless chatting
  • Creates urgency and intention
  • Pushes fast progression into real-world meeting
Connection type 05

BlindFate

A fully curated blind date experience. Flow

FateDate selects both the two people and the venue. Users do not need to plan anything. They simply arrive at the assigned venue at the arranged time.

Purpose
  • Maximum surprise
  • Zero planning friction
  • Fully curated real-world dating experience

How connections work

Once two users connect.

What unlocks

Once two users connect, they unlock:

  • Full profiles
  • Real names
  • Private chat
  • Ability to plan real-world dates
  • Profile interaction (likes, updates, engagement)

DateDiary — the real-world planning system

DateDiary is where connections become real plans. Once two users are ready to meet, in DateDiary they can:

  • Select or confirm a partnered venue
  • Choose date time and location
  • Set expectations for the date
  • Agree on payment style — split bill or pre-paid
  • Schedule an optional in-app call before meeting
  • Finalise all details in one place

This removes awkward planning, removes uncertainty, and turns intention into confirmed real-world dates quickly.

Before the date

Before the date.

Once connected, users can:

  • Chat freely
  • Get to know each other
  • Plan a call inside the app (optional)
  • Move directly into DateDiary planning when ready

This allows:

  • Either deeper connection first
  • Or fast progression to meeting
Real-world meeting

Real-world meeting.

All dates take place in:

  • Partnered, vetted venues
  • Safe, public environments designed for real conversation

Users are encouraged to meet:

  • In safe public settings
  • With clear expectations
  • With in-app safety support available throughout
After the date

After the date
outcome system.

Match

Continue seeing each other.

🤝

Keep light connection.

All outcomes are mutual in effect and intentional.
