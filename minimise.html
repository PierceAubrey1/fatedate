Safety, trust and structured dating by design

How we minimise the risks.

FateDate is a structured dating system, not an open-ended messaging platform.

Every interaction follows a clear progression: connection → intention → planning → real-world meeting → outcome.

Structured dating by design

Safety is not an add-on
it is built into the structure itself.

A system with direction

This structure is intentional. It exists to:

  • Reduce uncertainty and ambiguity
  • Prevent endless messaging
  • Improve real-world safety
  • Ensure every connection has direction

Built differently

Unlike traditional dating apps, FateDate is designed so interactions naturally move forward rather than stagnate.

Safety is not an add-on — it is built into the structure itself.

Identity & real users

Verified identity for every member.

ID verification

All users must complete identity verification before interacting on FateDate. This includes:

  • Government ID verification
  • Liveness / authenticity checks
  • Account approval before access

This ensures there are real, verified people behind every profile.

Live timestamp photo (anti-catfishing system)

To ensure profiles reflect real, current identity, users must upload a live in-app timestamped photo. This helps:

  • Confirm the user matches their profile
  • Prevent outdated or misleading images
  • Reduce catfishing and impersonation
  • Support informed decision-making before meeting

Unlike static profile images, this ensures identity is current and verified in real time.

Trust levels

FateDate uses a behavioural trust system based on:

  • Verification status
  • Consistent platform behaviour
  • Interaction history
  • Report activity (if any)

Trust levels help users assess reliability without exposing personal information.

Controlled connections & privacy

Your personal details stay yours.

Privacy by design

Users control how much of their identity is revealed at every stage.

  • Real names can remain hidden until chosen
  • No requirement to share phone numbers or social media
  • Messaging happens through FateDate ID

FateDate ID

FateDate ID allows users to:

  • Connect and chat without personal contact details
  • Maintain privacy until trust is established
  • Transition safely into real-world meetings

When meeting in person, users may choose to share their FateDate ID instead of phone numbers or social accounts.

Structured real-world dating flow

DateDiary
the core planning system.

DateDiary

DateDiary is where connections become structured real-world plans. Users can:

  • Select or propose a vetted venue
  • Set date time and location
  • Agree expectations before meeting
  • Choose payment setup (split or pre-paid)
  • Optionally schedule an in-app call before meeting

All venues are partnered and reviewed to ensure suitability for in-person dating.

This removes:

  • Vague planning
  • Last-minute confusion
  • Unsafe or unclear arrangements

Optional in-app call

Before meeting, users can:

  • Have a short in-app voice or video call
  • Build comfort and clarity
  • Confirm intent before meeting

This reduces uncertainty and improves confidence before real-world interaction.

After the date — outcomes

After meeting, both users independently choose an outcome:

  • Match — both users want to continue connecting. They become a match and unlock new experiences.
  • Friend connection — users decide to remain connected without romantic progression.
  • No further connection — the interaction ends cleanly with no obligation to continue.

This ensures outcomes are: mutual, clear, pressure-free.

Out & About mode (real-world discovery)

Real-world discovery, without the tracking risk.

How it works

Out & About allows users to reconnect with people they may have crossed paths with in real life.

  • Users may appear as a potential encounter after being in the same general area
  • Exact locations are never shared
  • No real-time tracking is ever exposed
  • Encounters are anonymised and privacy-protected

Daily updates

Encounter suggestions are updated once per day at midnight. This prevents live tracking or movement inference. Users only see past proximity, never current location.

This ensures:

  • No stalking risk
  • No live tracking
  • No location exposure
  • No behavioural surveillance
In-app safety tools

Tools that work when you need them.

Venue safety standards

All partnered venues are selected based on strict criteria:

  • Public, well-lit environments
  • Professional staff presence
  • Safe, appropriate settings for first meetings
  • Consistent operational standards

Private or unsafe meeting environments are not supported.

End Date Now

At any point during a date, users can activate End Date Now. This allows users to:

  • Leave discreetly and safely
  • Avoid confrontation
  • Exit without explanation
  • Trigger internal safety awareness signals

This ensures users always have a safe, pressure-free exit option.

Real-world safety guidance

Users are encouraged to:

  • Meet in public spaces
  • Avoid sharing home addresses early
  • Inform trusted contacts when meeting someone new
  • Use in-app safety tools where needed

FateDate reinforces safe behaviour throughout the experience.

Reporting & accountability

Safety is actively enforced, not assumed.

Reporting & moderation

Users can report:

  • Unsafe behaviour
  • Inappropriate conduct
  • Suspicious activity
  • Policy violations

All reports are:

  • Reviewed by moderation systems
  • Prioritised by severity
  • Actively enforced under clear community guidelines

Accountability & platform integrity

FateDate maintains:

  • Clear community guidelines
  • Active moderation systems
  • Behaviour-based enforcement
  • Removal of harmful users where necessary

Safety is actively enforced, not assumed.
