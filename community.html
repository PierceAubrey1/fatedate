Our Purpose

FateDate is a structured dating platform designed to move people from connection to real-world meetings with clarity, safety, and intention.

These Community Guidelines exist to protect users, set clear expectations, and ensure all interactions are respectful, accountable, and genuine, both online and in real life.

By creating an account or using FateDate, you agree to follow these guidelines.

1. Eligibility & Age Requirement

FateDate is strictly for users aged 18 or over.

All users must complete government-issued ID verification before accessing the platform.

Any attempt to access FateDate under the age of 18 will result in immediate and permanent removal.

Providing false or misleading information during verification or onboarding may result in account removal.

2. Account Integrity & Authenticity

Users must use their real identity. Only one account per person is permitted.

  • Account sharing, impersonation, or multiple accounts are prohibited
  • Profiles must remain accurate and up to date
  • Users may keep certain details private on public profiles, but must provide accurate information during verification

3. Trust Levels & Behaviour

FateDate uses a trust level system to reflect consistent, genuine behaviour across the platform.

Trust levels may be influenced by

  • Attending planned dates
  • Respectful interactions with other users
  • Profile consistency and accuracy
  • Reports or violations

Higher trust levels help other users make informed decisions.

Repeated negative behaviour may result in reduced trust levels, feature restrictions, or account removal. Low trust levels may also limit access to certain features, reduce visibility, or impact the ability to connect with other users.

4. Eligibility to Use FateDate

FateDate is intended for users who engage respectfully and safely.

Individuals who have engaged in violent, sexual, exploitative, or abusive behaviour are not permitted to use the platform.

Any user who violates these standards may have their account suspended or permanently removed.

5. Respect, Boundaries & Consent

All interactions must be respectful and consensual.

Users must not

  • Harass, threaten, intimidate, or demean others
  • Use hate speech or discriminatory language
  • Pressure or coerce others into conversation, calls, or dates
  • Ignore clearly stated boundaries

Consent can be withdrawn at any time and must always be respected.

6. Sexual Content & Conduct

Consensual flirting between adults is allowed. However, the following is not permitted:

  • Explicit or pornographic content
  • Unsolicited sexual messages or images
  • Sexual content involving coercion, exploitation, or violence
  • Any sexual content involving or referencing minors

Violations may result in immediate account removal.

7. Voice & Video Interactions

Voice and video interactions must remain respectful and appropriate.

Recording or sharing calls without consent is prohibited.

Any abusive or inappropriate behaviour during calls violates these guidelines.

8. BlindFate Dates

BlindFate is a structured blind date experience where FateDate selects both participants and the venue. As there is no prior connection or communication before the date:

  • Users must personally attend the date they are assigned
  • Substitutes or sending another person in your place are strictly prohibited
  • Failure to attend without proper cancellation may result in account restrictions or removal

BlindFate relies on trust, accountability, and showing up as agreed.

9. Date Planning, Attendance & Commitment

As part of FateDate's structured dating system, dates arranged through the platform — including those planned via DateDiary — represent a mutual commitment between both users.

Users are expected to attend dates as agreed. If unable to attend, users must cancel within the allowed timeframe. Repeated no-shows or late cancellations may result in reduced trust levels, feature restrictions, or account action.

Ending a date early — End Date Now

Any user may end a date at any time, including by using the End Date Now feature.

  • No explanation is required
  • The decision must be respected immediately
  • Pressuring someone to continue is a violation
  • Respectful behaviour is expected even if a date ends early

10. In-Person Date Conduct & Venue Safety

During in-person dates:

  • Users must respect personal space and boundaries at all times
  • Users must not follow another person after a date
  • Users must not attempt to move a date to a private location without clear mutual agreement
  • No one should feel pressured to leave the venue or continue a date
  • Users must not misrepresent themselves when attending a date (including appearance or identity)
  • Any behaviour that causes another user to feel unsafe may result in account action

Users are expected to meet directly at the agreed venue for first dates.

11. Date Costs & Financial Responsibility

Users should agree in advance how date costs will be handled.

If a user agrees to pay, they are expected to honour that agreement. Repeated failure to do so may be considered misuse of the platform.

FateDate does not mediate financial disputes but may take action where behaviour indicates bad faith.

12. Safety & Personal Information

For your safety, users must not

  • Share home addresses, workplace locations, or financial details publicly
  • Post phone numbers, emails, or social media handles on profiles
  • Pressure others to share personal details too early

Users are encouraged to

  • Use in-app messaging, voice, and video features
  • Use FateDate ID when meeting in real life instead of sharing personal contact details
  • Report any behaviour that feels unsafe or concerning

Behaviour that occurs off the platform but is connected to a FateDate interaction may still be considered when reviewing account safety and enforcement.

All communication on FateDate must remain within the platform until both users mutually agree to move to another form of contact.

13. Out & About Mode Safety

Out & About Mode is designed to enable real-world discovery while protecting user privacy.

  • Exact locations are never shared
  • Encounters are delayed and updated periodically (not in real time)
  • Users cannot track or identify precise movements

This ensures the feature remains safe, private, and non-intrusive.

14. Prohibited Activity

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Soliciting money, gifts, or financial assistance
  • Commercial activity or advertising
  • Scams, fraud, extortion, or blackmail
  • Drug-related activity or illegal substances
  • Violence or threats of violence
  • Any activity involving the exploitation of minors
  • Misuse of platform features in a way that disrupts, manipulates, or undermines the intended user experience

FateDate will cooperate with law enforcement where required.

15. Reporting & Enforcement

Users can report profiles, messages, calls, or behaviour that violates these guidelines.

FateDate may take action including:

  • Content removal
  • Feature restrictions
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account removal

Serious safety concerns may result in immediate account restriction while investigations are carried out.

16. Enforcement & Discretion

FateDate reserves the right to take action at its discretion to protect users and platform integrity.

Serious or repeated violations may result in permanent removal without notice.

17. Updates to These Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated to reflect safety improvements, legal requirements, or platform changes.

Continued use of FateDate constitutes acceptance of any updates.

Our Standard

FateDate is for people who show up, act honestly, respect boundaries, and date with intention.

Users who do not engage in good faith may lose access to the platform.
