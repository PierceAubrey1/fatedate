FAQs

Questions we hear a lot

Is there a cost to become a venue partner?

No. Being part of the core venue network is free. We also offer optional partnership packages for venues that want additional visibility, features, or enhanced placement in the app.

How do venues get used on FateDate?

Members either choose your venue when proposing a date, or in some cases FateDate assigns the venue to match the experience and ensure a curated, balanced date flow.

Do we need to change how we operate?

No. Bookings arrive like standard reservations. Your team does not need to change how service works.

What types of venues do you work with?

We work with a wide range of venues and spaces across the dating journey, from relaxed first date settings to more lively environments and experience-led venues for people continuing to see each other. The focus is on places that help people connect naturally, whatever stage they are at.

What makes a venue or experience a good fit?

We are selective about the venues and experiences we work with. Each one is chosen to deliver an enjoyable, unforgettable experience that fits the stage it is designed for.

Do you only work with first date venues?

No. We work with venues across different stages of connection. This includes first date spaces, ongoing date venues for people continuing to see each other, and select venues and experiences for more structured or memorable dates.

What are experiences?

Experiences are curated activities or environments designed for people who have already met, decided to match and want to continue their connection. These are part of the wider FateDate ecosystem beyond first dates.

How are venues chosen for a date?

For standard dates, members select a venue when proposing a date. For structured or blind date experiences, FateDate may assign a venue to create a guided experience.

How do bookings work?

Once a date is accepted and pre-paid, the booking is confirmed and sent to your venue via the FateDate dashboard like a standard reservation. No changes are required to your existing system.

How many bookings can we expect?

At launch, volumes will be modest while we grow the member base. Every booking comes from a verified, intentional member.

Can we pause our listing?

Yes. You can pause your listing at any time. Existing confirmed bookings will still go ahead, but no new bookings will be made while your venue is paused.

Where are you launching first?

We're rolling out across selected cities in 2026, starting with London and Manchester, with more locations to follow. Venue partnerships are being built in advance of each launch.
